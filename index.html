Yue Liu

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the mathematics department at Purdue University. I am currently working with Dr. Alexandria Volkening on biological pattern formation and agent-based modelling.
My mathematical interest includes dynamical systems and bifurcation theory, especially pattern formation in PDE models, as well as parameter inference and applications of machine learning. I am interested in applications in biology and fluid mechanics.

Previously, I completed my Doctor of Philosophy (DPhil) at the Mathematical Institute in the University of Oxford. I worked with Prof. Ruth Baker and Prof. Philip Maini on modelling of biological systems.

Before that, I have completed a Master of Science (MSc) at University of British Columbia, supervised by Prof. Leah Edelstein-Keshet.
